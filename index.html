Hi! My name is Jared Lorince
and I'm a socio-cognitive data scientist.

Thanks for visiting! I am a PhD candidate in cognitive science and psychology at Indiana University, Bloomington, working in the Adaptive Behavior and Cognition Lab. My work explores human behavior in social information environments on the Web, with a focus on music listening and tagging behavior. I integrate knowledge of human search and decision making capacities with large scale data mining and analysis methods.

Check out my research and CV pages for more detail on my work and experience, and my blog for updates, work-related and otherwise.
