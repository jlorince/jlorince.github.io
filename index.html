Hi! My name is Jared Lorince
and I'm a socio-cognitive data scientist.

(And I'm happy you decided to visit!)

I am a PhD student candidate in cognitive science and psychology at Indiana University, Bloomington, working in the Adaptive Behavior and Cognition Lab. My work explores human behavior in social information environments on the Web. This largely manifests in integrating knowledge of human search and decision making capacities with large scale data mining and analysis methods.
